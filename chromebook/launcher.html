Fyrecraft: Chromebook Edition (Tutorial)

For New Device Users

Step 1: Open your browser and type chrome://flags

Step 2: Search for JSPI

Step 3: Set JSPI to ENABLED

Step 4: Click Restart in the corner of your screen

Step 5: Press Launch Fyrecraft

For Users Who Already Did the Steps

Press launch fyrecraft

If you always get kicked, restart your device and close all tabs.
